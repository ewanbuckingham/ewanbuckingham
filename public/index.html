Photography, Climbing & Running

Ewan was born in Barrow-in-Furness in the North of England. He’s a graduate of the university of Warwick and has spent his professional career working in EdTech companies of all sizes. His professional biography is on LinkedIn.

Outside of work Ewan is a keen mountaineer, runner, cyclist and photographer. He’s travelled widely in the Europe, Scandinavia and North America writing and photographing his experiences.
